What subjects will I study in Mechanical Engineering?

Engineering Buddy

Great question! In Mechanical Engineering, you'll dive into core subjects like:

  • Thermodynamics: The study of heat and energy.
  • Fluid Mechanics: How liquids and gases behave.
  • Statics & Dynamics: The study of forces on objects.
  • Materials Science: Understanding the properties of different materials.

This foundation prepares you for exciting fields like robotics, automotive design, and aerospace!
